How to Update EduMocker App

Step 1: Open Google Chrome Browser on your mobile.
Step 2: Type and open this website:
edumocker.online
Step 3: After the website opens, tap the menu icon (☰) present at the top right corner.
Step 4: From the menu options, click on Update.
Step 5: A new page will open. Click on the Update option shown on that page.
Step 6: The download will start automatically and the app will be installed again with the latest version.
⚠️ Note: Please ensure you have a stable internet connection while updating the app.
